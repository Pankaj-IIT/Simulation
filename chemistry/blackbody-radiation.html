Temperature Control

Blackbody Radiation:
Wien's Law: λmax = b/T (b = 2.898×10⁻³ m·K)
Stefan-Boltzmann: I = σT⁴ (σ = 5.67×10⁻⁸ W/m²K⁴)
Higher temperature → shorter λmax → bluer color
Higher temperature → much higher total intensity

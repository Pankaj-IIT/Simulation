Millikan Oil Drop Experiment

Oil Drop Mass:1.0 × 10⁻¹⁵ kg
Net Force:0 N
State:Balanced
e (elementary charge):1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ C

Experiment Controls

Millikan Oil Drop Experiment (1909) measured the elementary charge of electron. By balancing gravitational and electric forces on oil drops, Millikan showed that charge is quantized: $q = ne$, where $n$ is an integer and $e = 1.6 \times 10^{-19}\text{ C}$.

Formula:
Relative accel = g + a
Coin rel pos = ut + ½(g+a)t²

Explanation:
From platform frame, coin feels (g+a).
- Upward accel (a > 0): coin hits faster
- Downward accel (a < 0): coin hits slower
- Throw up (u > 0): coin goes up first
- Throw down (u < 0): coin goes down faster

River Man Problem

River Velocity (vᵣ): 2.00 m/s
Man Velocity (vₘ): 3.00 m/s
Man Angle (θ): 90°
River Width (d): 30.0 m
Resultant Velocity: 3.61 m/s
Time to Cross: 10.00 s
Drift (Downstream): 20.00 m
Actual Distance: 36.06 m

Controls

Formula:
vₓ = vₘ cos θ + vᵣ
vᵧ = vₘ sin θ
vᵣₑₛ = √(vₓ² + vᵧ²)
Time = d / vᵧ
Drift = vₓ × time

Explanation:
- θ = 90°: man crosses straight across
- θ < 90°: man aims upstream
- θ > 90°: man aims downstream
- River pushes man downstream
